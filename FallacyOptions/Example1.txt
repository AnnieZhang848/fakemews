Strawman
////
Person A wants to shut down all industries and put everyone out of work.
//// 
This is a classic strawman fallacy.
In this case, Person B misrepresents Person A's position by exaggerating it (implying they want to shut down all industries), 
instead of addressing the actual point about stricter environmental regulations.
